### Hybrid Scoring

```
final_score = 0.5 * normalized_bm25_score + 0.5 * semantic_similarity
```

- **BM25**: Exact keyword matching (traditional search)
- **Semantic**: Meaning-based matching (embeddings)
- **Hybrid**: Best of both worlds
