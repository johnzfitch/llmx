### 3. Reciprocal Rank Fusion (RRF)

Replaced linear score combination with RRF for merging BM25 and semantic results.

**Advantages:**
- More robust across different score scales
- No normalization required
- Boosts consensus results (appearing in multiple ranked lists)

**Formula:**
```
RRF(document) = Î£ 1/(k + rank(document))
where k = 60 (standard constant)
```

**Implementation:** `ingestor-core/src/rrf.rs`
